Meet the Analyst

Introduction

I am a meticulous data analyst passionate about uncovering data-driven trends and using data to deepen my understanding of the world. I thrive on the challenge of identifying patterns and transforming complex datasets into actionable insights that drive strategic decisions. When I'm not conducting analyses, you can find me (coffee in hand) creating fun personal spreadsheets or working on my current crochet and knitting projects.

Professional Journey

My journey to data analysis began with my passion for understanding trends, a skill I honed during my time as a science teacher. I regularly analyzed student performance data to adapt my lessons and ensure student success. Currently, I utilize those analytical skills to create compelling data visualizations for a medical doctor and author, ensuring readers understand experimental and research findings through powerful visualizations.
